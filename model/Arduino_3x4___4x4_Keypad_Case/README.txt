# Summary

A simple 3x4 & 4x4 Keypad case for arduino/nodemcu/raspberry pi projects.

Please be aware that this is NOT a "high security" case, it's just for "low security" projects.

Don't blame on me if your home gets broken into.

# Post-Printing

## Recommendations:

I highly recommend to put ONLY the wires inside this case,  Do not put the electronics that activates the latch inside this case.  

Pretty much you have to put outside the keypad and the electronics INSIDE of the place you want to protect.
